!<INPUT 0>! -- number of players
!<INPUT 1>! -- ratio string
!<INPUT 2>! -- maximum utility
!<INPUT 3>! -- minimum utility
!<INPUT 4>! -- utility of staying home
!<INPUT 5>! -- round message

<commentblockmarker>###</commentblockmarker>

A group of people, which includes you, aspire to visit a bar. The venue has a restricted capacity, therefore, the ambience remains pleasant if it's not overly packed. Particularly, the utility of those in attendance decreases to !<INPUT 2>! if the crowd surpasses the !<INPUT 1>! capacity mark. However, if the number of individuals remains at or below !<INPUT 1>!, those joining the bar realize a utility of !<INPUT 3>!. People who choose to stay home, on the other hand, enjoy a set utility of !<INPUT 4>!. What's interesting is that everyone makes their choice to attend or not concurrently, without any prior information of what others have chosen. !<INPUT 5>! All you aim is getting the most out of your utility.
