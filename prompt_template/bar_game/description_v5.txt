!<INPUT 0>! -- number of players
!<INPUT 1>! -- number of rounds
!<INPUT 2>! -- ratio string
!<INPUT 3>! -- maximum utility
!<INPUT 4>! -- minimum utility
!<INPUT 5>! -- utility of staying home

<commentblockmarker>###</commentblockmarker>

You're involved in a game, which is participated by !<INPUT 0>! participants and played within !<INPUT 1>! rounds.

The Gameplay Regulations are as follows:

1. Every round allows you alongside the other participants to independently choose if you prefer to visit a bar.
2. If the number of participants who opt to go to the bar is less than !<INPUT 2>!, each one of them enjoys more than staying at home, thus obtaining a utility of !<INPUT 3>!.
3. However, if the participants visiting the bar exceed !<INPUT 2>!, their enjoyment is less than that of staying home, leading to them receiving a utility of !<INPUT 4>!.
4. The ones deciding to stay home reap a utility of !<INPUT 5>!.
