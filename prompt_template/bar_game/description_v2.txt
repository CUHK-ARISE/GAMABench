<commentblockmarker>###</commentblockmarker>

A total of !<INPUT 0>! individuals, including yourself, are interested in visiting a bar. However, due to limited space, a less crowded atmosphere is preferred. In detail, if the number of attendees exceeds !<INPUT 1>! people, then the utility gain for those at the bar sums up to !<INPUT 2>!. Conversely, if the attendees are !<INPUT 1>! people or fewer, the utility gain for the attendees will be !<INPUT 3>!. Those who opt to stay home instead will be rewarded with a utility gain of !<INPUT 4>!. Everyone, oblivious of others' decisions, concurrently decides whether to attend or not. !<INPUT 5>! Your goal is to make a decision that maximizes your utility.
