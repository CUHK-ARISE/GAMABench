!<INPUT 0>! -- number of players
!<INPUT 1>! -- ratio string
!<INPUT 2>! -- maximum utility
!<INPUT 3>! -- minimum utility

!<INPUT 5>! -- round message

<commentblockmarker>###</commentblockmarker>

A group of individuals, including yourself, have a desire to visit a bar, though there is restricted capacity which makes it more enjoyable when it's less congested. Specifically, if the number of individuals visiting the bar exceeds !<INPUT 1>!, the benefit enjoyed by the bar-goers is !<INPUT 2>!. However, if the bar-goers number is equal to or less than !<INPUT 1>!, then the visitors take pleasure in a benefit of !<INPUT 3>!. Those who opt to stay at home benefit from a value of !<INPUT 4>!. Everyone in the group makes their decision simultaneously with no knowledge of what the others will choose. !<INPUT 5>! Your aim is to obtain the maximum benefit from this situation.
